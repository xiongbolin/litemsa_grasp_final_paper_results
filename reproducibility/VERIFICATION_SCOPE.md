# Verification scope

The public verifier checks manuscript-facing aggregate values, recomputes the 48 recorded tolerance decisions, confirms the Figure 3-5 source-data inventory, and preserves the reported negative confidence-comparison result.

It does not claim to reproduce the original model-training runtime, third-party datasets, model checkpoints, or physical-robot experiments. The provided `requirements.txt` and `environment.yml` define a public audit and plotting environment only.
