# Reproducibility scope

## Directly supported by this repository

- Inspection of selected configuration files.
- Recalculation of headline values from preserved aggregate result tables.
- Audit of the post-hoc soft-reliability and confidence-only comparison tables.
- Inspection of the manuscript source and claim boundaries.

## Not supported by the supplied historical archive

- End-to-end model retraining from raw Cornell or Jacquard data.
- Exact regeneration of every checkpoint and per-sample prediction.
- Independent verification of the original hardware/software runtime environment.
- Physical-robot grasp-success validation.
- Strict Cornell object-wise evaluation based on an authoritative image-to-object map.

No missing code, checkpoint, environment, split identity, or experimental result has been reconstructed without evidence. The manuscript therefore makes bounded robot-free claims and identifies these limitations explicitly.
